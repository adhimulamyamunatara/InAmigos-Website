About The Foundation

Creating positive social impact across India

Uniting Minds For Change

InAmigos Foundation is a non-profit organization dedicated to social welfare, community development, education, women empowerment, youth skill development, environmental sustainability, food distribution and volunteer-driven impact.

Through various campaigns and projects, the foundation continuously works to inspire hope, leadership, compassion and positive transformation in society.
